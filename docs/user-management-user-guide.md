# User Management - User Guide

## Overview

This guide is for regular users of Pimify, covering how to manage your account, update your profile, and use user management features.

## Account Access

### Logging In

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click **Login**

If you have two-factor authentication enabled:
5. Enter 2FA code or backup code

### Forgot Password

1. Click **Forgot Password** on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Click **Send Reset Link**
4. Check your email for password reset link
5. Click link and enter new password
6. Log in with new password

**Note:** Password reset links expire after 1 hour.

### First Time Login

If you're logging in for the first time after receiving an invitation:
1. Click the invitation link in your email
2. Set your password (must meet password requirements)
3. Optionally complete your profile
4. Click **Create Account**
5. You'll be automatically logged in

## Profile Management

### Viewing Your Profile

1. Click your name/avatar in the top navigation
2. View your profile information:
   - Name, email, role
   - Department, job title
   - Phone, location, timezone
   - Avatar image
   - Account status

### Editing Your Profile

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click **Edit Profile**
3. Update fields:
   - Name
   - Phone
   - Location
   - Timezone
   - Bio
   - Avatar
4. Click **Save**

**Note:** You cannot change your email or role. Contact an administrator.

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click **Change Password**
3. Enter current password
4. Enter new password (must meet requirements)
5. Confirm new password
6. Click **Change Password**

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter
- At least one lowercase letter
- At least one number
- At least one special character
- Cannot be same as previous 5 passwords

### Uploading Avatar

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click on your avatar
3. Select image file (JPG, PNG, GIF)
4. Crop/resize if needed
5. Click **Save**

**File Requirements:**
- Max size: 10 MB
- Formats: JPG, PNG, GIF
- Recommended: Square image, 200x200 pixels or larger

## Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

### Enabling 2FA

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click **Security** tab
3. Click **Enable 2FA**
4. Scan QR code with authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
5. Enter verification code
6. Save backup codes securely
7. Click **Enable**

**Important:** Save backup codes in a secure location. You'll need them if you lose access to your authenticator app.

### Disabling 2FA

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click **Security** tab
3. Click **Disable 2FA**
4. Enter verification code
4. Confirm disable

## Sessions

### Viewing Active Sessions

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click **Security** tab
3. View **Active Sessions**:
   - Current device (highlighted)
   - Other devices
   - Last active time
   - IP address
   - Location

### Terminating Sessions

1. Go to **Active Sessions**
2. Find session to terminate
3. Click **Terminate** on specific session
4. Or click **Terminate All** to log out from all devices

**Note:** Terminating all sessions will log you out everywhere except your current device.

## Notifications

### Notification Preferences

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click **Notifications** tab
3. Configure preferences:
   - Email notifications
   - In-app notifications
   - Notification types:
     - Product assignments
     - Review requests
     - System updates
     - Security alerts
4. Click **Save**

### Viewing Notifications

1. Click notification bell icon in navigation
2. View unread notifications
3. Mark as read by clicking
4. Clear all notifications

## Activity Log

### Viewing Your Activity

1. Go to your profile page
2. Click **Activity** tab
3. View activity timeline:
   - Login/logout events
   - Profile changes
   - Product actions
   - Permission changes
4. Filter by:
   - Date range
   - Activity type
   - Action

## If You're a Reviewer

### Reviewer Dashboard

Access **Reviewers** page to see:
- Your current workload
- Pending reviews
- Performance metrics
- Your availability status

### Setting Availability

1. Go to **Reviewers** page
2. Find yourself in the list
3. Click **Set Availability**
4. Select status:
   - Available
   - Busy
   - Away
   - Vacation
5. Set date range (optional)
6. Click **Save**

### Reviewer Metrics

View your performance:
- Reviews completed
- Average review time
- Approval rate
- Rating

## Account Issues

### Account Locked

If your account is locked (too many failed login attempts):
1. Wait 15 minutes for auto-unlock
2. Or contact administrator to unlock
3. Reset password if needed

### Can't Access Account

1. Try password reset
2. Check email for notifications
3. Verify account is active
4. Contact administrator

### Permission Issues

If you can't access a feature:
1. Check your role permissions
2. Verify you're logged in with correct account
3. Contact administrator to verify permissions

## Security Tips

1. **Strong Passwords**
   - Use unique, complex passwords
   - Don't reuse passwords
   - Change password regularly

2. **Two-Factor Authentication**
   - Enable 2FA for additional security
   - Keep backup codes safe

3. **Session Management**
   - Log out from shared computers
   - Review active sessions regularly
   - Terminate suspicious sessions

4. **Email Security**
   - Verify email address is correct
   - Report suspicious emails
   - Don't share account credentials

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I change my email address?

Contact an administrator. Email addresses cannot be changed by users for security reasons.

### Can I have multiple accounts?

No, each email address can only have one account.

### What if I forget my 2FA backup codes?

Contact an administrator to reset your 2FA. You'll need to set it up again.

### How long are sessions active?

Sessions expire after 60 minutes of inactivity or 24 hours total, whichever comes first.

### Can I delete my account?

Contact an administrator to deactivate or delete your account.

### What happens when my password expires?

You'll be prompted to change your password on next login.

## Best Practices

1. Keep your profile information up to date
2. Use strong, unique passwords
3. Enable two-factor authentication
4. Review your activity log regularly
5. Report suspicious activity immediately
6. Keep notification preferences configured
7. Log out from shared devices
